### 2. Key Technical Implementations
*   **Model Fine-tuning (LoRA)**: To optimize resource usage, I fine-tuned `sshleifer/distilbart-cnn-12-6` and `t5-small` using Hugging Face PEFT/LoRA adapters on the BillSum legal dataset. This achieved rapid convergence and aligned the models with legal phrasing and contract structures.
*   **Long-Input Chunking Strategy**: Implemented an overlapping chunking pipeline (chunk size: 1024 tokens, overlap: 100 tokens) coupled with a map-reduce aggregation scheme to process long agreements without encountering encoder context limits.
*   **Clause Preservation Tagger**: Designed a token-overlap and regex clause tagging system focusing on the five most critical clauses requested (Confidentiality, Governing Law, Termination, Indemnification, Intellectual Property) to track retention rates across the various summarizers.
*   **Extractive Baseline**: Implemented a LexRank baseline using the `sumy` framework to serve as a comparative standard against our abstractive models.
*   **Evaluation Metrics**: Computed and compiled ROUGE-1, ROUGE-2, and ROUGE-L scores along with custom Clause Preservation Rates for 10 contracts, outputting the metrics to `outputs/summaries/ten_contract_results.json`.
